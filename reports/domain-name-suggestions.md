## Alternative Domain Extension Strategies

If .com domains are unavailable or too expensive:

### Premium Design Extensions
- **.design** - Most relevant for this project
- **.dev** - Developer-friendly, modern
- **.tools** - Direct and descriptive
- **.studio** - Creative and professional
- **.io** - Tech-savvy, modern

### Creative Combinations
If single words are unavailable:
- **use[word].com** (e.g., usepalette.com)
- **get[word].com** (e.g., getprism.com)
- **[word]hq.com** (e.g., palettehq.com)
- **try[word].com** (e.g., trycurate.com)

## Domain Availability Considerations

Before finalizing any domain:

1. **Check availability** across all major extensions (.com, .design, .dev, .tools)
2. **Verify trademarks** to avoid legal issues
3. **Social media handles** - Ensure Twitter, GitHub, etc. are available
4. **SEO implications** - Consider search volume and competition
5. **Budget** - Premium domains can range from $10 to $100,000+
